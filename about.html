Our History

WolfPack Sustainability Organization was founded in 2025 by students passionate about environmental action. We organize cleanup drives, recycling campaigns, and awareness events across campus and Raleigh.

Who We Are

We are open to all students — no experience required. Members come from all majors and share a common goal of protecting the environment while building community.

Official Member List

Official Members are individuals who have demonstrated consistent involvement and commitment to Students for Sustainability. Members earn this designation by attending at least two cleanup events and two General Body Meetings, recognizing their active participation and support of our organization's mission.
