Our History

The Students for Sustainability was founded in 2025 by students passionate about environmental action. We plan on organizing cleanup drives, recycling campaigns, and awareness events across campus and Raleigh.

Who We Are

We are open to all students and no experience is needed! We’re a group of students from all majors who care about nature and want to make a difference together.
